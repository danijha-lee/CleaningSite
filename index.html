About Us

Mo's Cleaning Servicehas prepared for the return of workers, shoppers, students and visitors, we are ready to help you ensure they have confidence in the safety of their cleaned environment. Our Level Up cleaning program creates safety-first environments through stringent processes, innovative technologies, and effective cleaning products and is highly visible. Our employees are trained in the latest practices and protocols, and can assist any organization, big or small, through this transitionary phase. For every client and site, Mo's Cleaning Service now incorporates best practices quickly instituted during the early days of the COVID-19 outbreak. We are keeping pace in real-time with new governmental directives for PPE, cleaning products, and distancing protocols to ensure the safety of our employees and our clients’ occupants. All of our clients benefit from:

  • * Cleaners and technicians trained on the latest methods to ensure compliant and safe results.

  • *Proactive leverage of supply relationships for the availability of supplies even in times of scarcity, including products on the EPA’s List N, and PPE.

  • * Centralized waste removal services to contain potential contaminants.
